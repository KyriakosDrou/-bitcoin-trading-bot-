STAR TANKERS LLC
DIVIDEND RESOLUTION

Resolution Date: 30th June 2025

RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS/MEMBERS
INTERIM DIVIDEND 2025 - CROWNSAIL PARTNERS LIMITED

WHEREAS, Star Tankers LLC has generated sufficient profits and cash flow to support a dividend distribution; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors/Members deems it appropriate to declare and pay an interim dividend for the year 2025;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that:

1. Declaration of Dividend: Star Tankers LLC hereby declares an interim dividend for the year 2025 in the amount of $1,295,774.64 (One Million Two Hundred Ninety-Five Thousand Seven Hundred Seventy-Four US Dollars and Sixty-Four Cents).

2. Recipient: The dividend shall be paid to Crownsail Partners Limited.

3. Payment Date: The dividend shall be paid no later than 25th July 2025.

4. Authorization: The officers of Star Tankers LLC are hereby authorized and directed to take all necessary actions to effectuate the payment of this dividend, including but not limited to:
   - Preparing and executing all necessary documentation
   - Making the required accounting entries
   - Transferring the funds to Crownsail Partners Limited

5. Compliance: This dividend payment is made in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and the operating agreement of Star Tankers LLC.

6. Record Keeping: A copy of this resolution shall be maintained in the corporate records of Star Tankers LLC.

ADOPTED this 30th day of June, 2025.
