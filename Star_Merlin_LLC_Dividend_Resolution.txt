STAR MERLIN LLC
DIVIDEND RESOLUTION

Resolution Date: 30th June 2025

RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS/MEMBERS
INTERIM DIVIDEND 2024

WHEREAS, Star Merlin LLC has generated sufficient profits and cash flow to support a dividend distribution; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors/Members deems it appropriate to declare and pay an interim dividend for the year 2024;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that:

1. Declaration of Dividend: Star Merlin LLC hereby declares an interim dividend for the year 2024 in the amount of $220,000 (Two Hundred Twenty Thousand US Dollars).

2. Recipient: The dividend shall be paid to Star Tankers LLC, being the sole member/shareholder of Star Merlin LLC.

3. Payment Date: The dividend shall be paid no later than 25th July 2025.

4. Authorization: The officers of Star Merlin LLC are hereby authorized and directed to take all necessary actions to effectuate the payment of this dividend, including but not limited to:
   - Preparing and executing all necessary documentation
   - Making the required accounting entries
   - Transferring the funds to Star Tankers LLC

5. Compliance: This dividend payment is made in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and the operating agreement of Star Merlin LLC.

6. Record Keeping: A copy of this resolution shall be maintained in the corporate records of Star Merlin LLC.

ADOPTED this 30th day of June, 2025.
