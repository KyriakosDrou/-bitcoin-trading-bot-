STAR EAGLE LLC
DIVIDEND RESOLUTION

Resolution Date: 30th June 2025

RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS/MEMBERS
FINAL DIVIDEND 2023 AND INTERIM DIVIDEND 2024

WHEREAS, Star Eagle LLC has generated sufficient profits and cash flow to support dividend distributions; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors/Members deems it appropriate to declare and pay a final dividend for the year 2023 and an interim dividend for the year 2024;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that:

1. Declaration of Dividends: Star Eagle LLC hereby declares:
   a) Final Dividend 2023 in the amount of $856,370 (Eight Hundred Fifty-Six Thousand Three Hundred Seventy US Dollars)
   b) Interim Dividend 2024 in the amount of $933,630 (Nine Hundred Thirty-Three Thousand Six Hundred Thirty US Dollars)
   c) Total dividend payment of $1,790,000 (One Million Seven Hundred Ninety Thousand US Dollars)

2. Recipient: The dividends shall be paid to Star Tankers LLC, being the sole member/shareholder of Star Eagle LLC.

3. Payment Date: The dividends shall be paid no later than 25th July 2025.

4. Authorization: The officers of Star Eagle LLC are hereby authorized and directed to take all necessary actions to effectuate the payment of these dividends, including but not limited to:
   - Preparing and executing all necessary documentation
   - Making the required accounting entries
   - Transferring the funds to Star Tankers LLC

5. Compliance: These dividend payments are made in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and the operating agreement of Star Eagle LLC.

6. Record Keeping: A copy of this resolution shall be maintained in the corporate records of Star Eagle LLC.

ADOPTED this 30th day of June, 2025.
